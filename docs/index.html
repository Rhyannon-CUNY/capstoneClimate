How has your climate changed?

By Rhyannon Bartlett-Imadegawa Published [Date]

Winters are warmer and weather disasters are happening more often. But by how much?

My grandmother turned 100-years-old this year. She was born before Marilyn Monroe and Queen Elizabeth II.

Summer are so hot now that she can't leave the house.

The climate has changed a lot in her lifetime.

What will it be like when you are 100? And how much has it changed since you were born?
